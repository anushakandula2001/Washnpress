N E X T S T E P S
From Identity to Launch
Recommended actions to take the brand from concept to live
01
Finalise Logo
Choose between Concept A, B, or C. Commission a vector artist to
build the full SVG mark with clear-space guidelines and all approved
colour variants.
02
Register Trademark
File WnP wordmark + mark under Class 37 (laundry services) with
India's Trade Marks Registry. Simultaneously register the .in and .com
domains.
03
Brand Guidelines
Compile a single PDF covering: logo usage, colour codes
(HEX/CMYK/RGB), typography scale, photography style, and icon
library.
04
Build Website MVP
Homepage, Plans, For Societies, and Contact pages. Static site
(Next.js) deploying to Vercel. Link to app store pages once live.
05
Society Launch Kit
RWA pitch deck, printed signage templates, laundry-bag artwork file,
and welcome-letter template — all using the finalised brand.
